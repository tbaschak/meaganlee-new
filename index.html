About Meagan

On Location. Classy. Fine Arts.

Meagan is at heart, a perfectionist -- and it shows in the quality of her work. Both in composition, and post processing. She will do everything within her power to provide the best experience to you, whether that be in studio, or on location.

Meagan has seven years experience as a wedding photographer. Having worked with (and around) many great names in Winnipeg, she brings her own classicly influenced style to her work.
